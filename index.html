The Blind Spot

The gap between ERP records and financial reality

Most organisations have two pictures of their payments: what their systems believe happened, and what actually happened in the bank account. ERP, AP automation, vendor master files and TPRM platforms are built around the approved vendor list. They struggle to surface entities that receive funds without ever being set up as vendors.

Picture 1: The ERP Illusion

The system tells you every vendor is validated, tracked, and vetted. AP automation workflows appear perfectly sound because they only process what enters the pipeline. Sampling audits capture standard variances but miss entirely bypassed controls.

Result: Blind trust in incomplete records.

Picture 2: Payment Reality

The bank account reveals shadow entities and ghost vendors collecting multi-million dollar payouts entirely off the radar. These legitimate-looking recipients sit completely outside formal procurement, TPRM workflows, and master files.

Result: Realized financial loss and unquantified risk.

Audit Pressure

Boards and audit committees increasingly ask for independent proof of who actually received payment, not just ERP reports.

Loss Exposure

Ghost vendor schemes and social‑engineering frauds continue to hit public bodies and enterprises with heavy losses.

The Insight

Where AI SecureWatch sits in the stack

AI SecureWatch is an enterprise intelligence layer focused on “ground truth” for payments — ingesting AP files and the vendor master to produce a reconciled view of every entity that actually received money, and flagging those that are not in the master file.
